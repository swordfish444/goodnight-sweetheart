# Terms of Use for Goodnight Sweetheart

Goodnight Sweetheart is provided as a bedtime reminder and wellness experience. By enabling or using the skill, the customer agrees to use it through supported Alexa devices and Alexa reminder surfaces.

## Intended use

Goodnight Sweetheart is designed to help customers schedule affectionate bedtime reminders. It is not medical advice, mental health advice, crisis support, or emergency assistance.

## Availability

The skill is provided on an as-is and as-available basis. Reminder delivery depends on Alexa services, device availability, permissions granted by the customer, and the customer's device settings such as time zone configuration.

## Customer controls

Customers control the schedules they create through the skill. Customers can ask Alexa to change or clear reminders at any time.

## Limitation of use

The skill should not be used for emergency, safety-critical, or time-sensitive obligations where a missed reminder could cause harm.
